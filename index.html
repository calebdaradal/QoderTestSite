About Maya

With over 8 years of experience in digital art and illustration, I specialize in creating captivating fantasy worlds, memorable characters, and atmospheric environments. My work spans from concept art for indie games to book cover illustrations and personal artistic projects.

I'm passionate about storytelling through visual art, combining traditional artistic principles with cutting-edge digital techniques to bring imagination to life. Every piece I create aims to transport viewers into new worlds and evoke deep emotional connections.
